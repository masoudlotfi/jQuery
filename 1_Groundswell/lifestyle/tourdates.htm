Team Groundswell Road Tour

For those of you that hung out with us last year, welcome back! Man we had an absolute blast and we're looking forward to getting on the road again.

Joining us on the road this year are newcomers Marty Fleming on the Men's side and Jen Truscott on the women's side. We're looking forward to seeing what these folks can do on tour.

Don't forget to submit your favorite appearances from last year. We've got a stack a mile long and we'll be releasing a highlight video later this month.

We're looking forward to hanging with each and every one of you!

Team Groundswell Road Tour

Men's Dates | Women's Dates

Come join us on the road...you know you want to! Some days we'll just be relaxing and surfing, other days we'll be in the thick of a competition. No matter what we'd love hanging out with you. Be sure to check out all the cool swag we have too!

If you really like hanging out with us, we have tryouts each year at the start of the surf season. Tryouts generally last for three weeks as new team members are chosen. Check for the tryout dates and location to be listed about one month before the scheduled date.
